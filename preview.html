Mathematics·Algebra·Lesson 3

Solving simultaneous equations.

When you see two equations with two unknowns, UNEB wants you to eliminate one variable first. The marking scheme awards method marks even if your final answer slips, so always show your working, step by step.

Worked example
1Label the equations. Make the coefficients of one variable equal in both.
2Add or subtract the equations to eliminate that variable.
3Solve for the remaining unknown, then substitute back to find the other.
